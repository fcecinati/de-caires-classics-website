912R is Umberto's masterpiece s far. R stands for Rust (Ruggine), because it came to us as a rusty and neglected car. We restored and rebuilt everything, from the chassis to the headlights, from the engine to the handbreak handle, passing by all the mechanics, bodywork, interiors, details. The 912 has been restored with fidelity to the spirit of the 1960's but with some mdern twists, improving performance, but maintaining the feel. We expect to complete 912R in 2026.
