01

912R

R is for Ruggine: Italian for rust. The car arrived heavily corroded and long neglected. The scope of the restoration expanded as the work progressed, and eventually it covered everything: chassis, engine, gearbox, brakes, suspension, bodywork, interior, every fitting and detail.

The 912 has been rebuilt with fidelity to its 1960s character, with considered improvements to performance. The goal was not a concours reconstruction but a car built to be driven, perhaps better than it ever was.

Work is ongoing. Expected completion: 2026.
